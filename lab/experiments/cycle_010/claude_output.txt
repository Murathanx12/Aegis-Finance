## Cycle 10 Summary

**What I found:** Two parameters in `get_market_signal()` were computed by all 3 router endpoints (stock screener, stock signal, market signal) but silently discarded in the function body:
- `risk_score` — the 9-factor composite macro risk score (VIX, yield curve, credit spreads, etc.)
- `crash_prob_12m` — the 12-month crash probability

**What I did:**

1. **Added `macro_risk` as a 7th signal component** (12% weight) in `signal_engine.py`:
   - Maps risk_score [-4, +4] → signal [-1.0, +0.5] via linear transform
   - High stress (risk_score=3.0) → -0.75 (strong bearish tilt)
   - Neutral (0) → 0, low stress (-2) → +0.5

2. **Blended `crash_prob_12m` into the crash component**: 70% weight on 3m (near-term), 30% on 12m (structural risk)

3. **Rebalanced signal weights** in `config.py` to accommodate the new component while keeping sum=1.00

**Results:**
- Stressed market composite score drops from ~-0.27 to -0.365 (more appropriately bearish)
- Normal market crash component refined from 0.50 (3m only) to 0.44 (blended with 12m=25%)
- All 60 fast tests pass, no regressions
- Only 1 dead parameter remains (`sp500_ytd_return`)
