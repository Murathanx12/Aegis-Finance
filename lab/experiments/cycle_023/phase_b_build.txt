7. **`test_hmm_regime.py`** — 12 new tests covering HMM fitting, MC integration, fallbacks, and drift detector reproducibility

### Results: **227 passed, 0 failed** (up from 215)

### Why this matters:
The MC engine already had `hmm_drift_blend=0.15` and `hmm_vol_blend=0.15` in config, plus full HMM blending code in `simulate_paths()` — but nothing ever called the HMM model. Now every simulation gets probabilistic regime conditioning, producing drift/vol that adapts to the current market state rather than relying solely on rule-based thresholds.
