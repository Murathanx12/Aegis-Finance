### What changed numerically

Before the fix, `sector_mu = ln(1+r)` was missing the `- 0.5σ²` Ito term. The bias per sector:

| Sector (approx σ) | Annual drift bias |
|---|---|
| Energy (25%) | +3.1% |
| Technology (22%) | +2.4% |
| Financials (20%) | +2.0% |
| Consumer Staples (14%) | +1.0% |

Over 5 years, that compounds to **+10-17% inflated returns** for high-vol sectors. The fix makes `sim_total_return` (MC) track `expected_total` (factor model) as intended.
