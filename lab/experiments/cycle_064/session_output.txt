## Relative Valuation & Peer Comparison (Koyfin-style)

**New service:** `backend/services/relative_valuation.py`

Ranks any stock against its sector peers on **13 valuation metrics**:
- P/E (trailing & forward), PEG ratio, EV/EBITDA
- P/S, P/B, dividend yield, FCF yield
- Revenue growth, earnings growth, profit margin, ROE, D/E

**What users see:**
- **Percentile rankings** within sector (e.g., "AAPL's P/E is at the 35th percentile in Technology")
- **Composite valuation score** (0-100, weighted across all metrics)
- **Verdict labels**: Deep Value / Undervalued / Fair Value / Overvalued / Significantly Overvalued
- **Historical P/E context**: current vs 5-year range (e.g., "P/E near 5-year lows")
- **Peer comparison table** with all metrics side-by-side
- **Human-readable interpretations** ("significantly cheaper", "premium", "in line")

**Integration points:**
- `GET /api/stock/{ticker}/valuation` — full peer comparison view
- `GET /api/stock/{ticker}` — now includes `relative_valuation` summary in response
- Frontend: `getStockValuation()` + full TypeScript types
