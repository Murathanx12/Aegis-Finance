## Cross-Asset Macro Regime Monitor

A Bloomberg MAC3-style cross-asset intelligence service with 6 components:

**1. Growth × Inflation Quadrant** — Classifies the macro environment into 4 regimes:
- **Goldilocks** (growth up, inflation down) — risk assets favored
- **Reflation** (both up) — commodities and cyclicals outperform
- **Stagflation** (growth down, inflation up) — worst for portfolios
- **Deflation** (both down) — treasuries rally, risk-off

Growth proxy: HYG/LQD credit ratio + IWM/SPY size ratio + EEM/EFA EM ratio
Inflation proxy: TIP/IEF breakeven + gold momentum + oil momentum

**2. Risk-On/Risk-Off Score** (0-100) from 6 z-scored cross-asset signals (equity momentum, credit appetite, safe haven demand, USD strength, volatility, EM appetite)

**3. Multi-timeframe Momentum Table** — 15 assets across equities, bonds, commodities, currencies, and crypto with 1w/1m/3m/6m/1y returns + SMA200 trend

**4. Rolling Correlation Matrix** with divergence detection vs historical norms

**5. Intermarket Divergence Alerts** — 5 signal types (stock-bond correlation flip, gold/USD co-movement, credit-equity divergence, small-cap underperformance, EM-DM divergence)

**6. Macro Weather Summary** — Plain-English one-liner combining all signals
