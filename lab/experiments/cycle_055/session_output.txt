## R&D Cycle 055 Results

### Bugs Fixed (3)
1. **Signal engine weights** — `economic_surprise` and `momentum_breadth` signals were computed and stored in components but had **zero weight** in the composite (missing from `config.signal_weights` and all regime weight profiles). 2. **Bubble detector discontinuity** — `get_bubble_signal_score` jumped from -0.25 to -0.5 at the confidence threshold. The partial warning zone now ramps continuously to -0.5.

3. **Portfolio analysis gap** — `analyze_portfolio` returned basic metrics but lacked the Bloomberg PORT-style analytics that the attribution service already supported.

- Performance attribution vs SPY (allocation, selection, interaction effects)
- Per-asset Marginal Contribution to Risk (weight, MCTR, risk contribution %, risk/weight ratio)
