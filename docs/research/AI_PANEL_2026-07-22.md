# AI panel synthesis — ChatGPT vs Gemini vs DeepSeek on "build the brain, beat SPY"
*(2026-07-22. Murat pasted three AI responses to his manifesto; this is the
adjudication — what we adopt, what we reject, and why. Companion protocol:
`Aegis module/docs/STRATEGY_FACTORY.md`.)*

## Where all three agree (and we adopt)

1. **No end-to-end neural net on raw prices.** All three, independently. This
   matches Gu-Kelly-Xiu (2020): ML's gains come from nonlinear combinations of
   *economically grounded* characteristics, not from a big model discovering
   patterns in raw data. Our architecture already does this (signals → shallow
   ranker); no CNN will be built.
2. **The LLM is the perception layer, never the allocator.** Extract events,
   summarize filings, link entities, explain decisions, generate hypotheses —
   don't size positions. Already CANON ("LLM never allocates"); the panel
   unanimously confirms it.
3. **Strategy factory over one-great-strategy.** Maintain a large library of
   simple, falsifiable hypotheses; test at scale; let survivors earn weight.
   ADOPTED — built 2026-07-22 as the explore/confirm factory.
4. **Alt-data is the retail-accessible edge zone**: insider clusters, FDA
   events, congress, supply-chain links, revisions — not price factors. Matches
   our own trial history (price factors REJECT ×3; insider = first survivor).
5. **The product is valuable even if it never beats SPY.** "Bloomberg sells
   information; you sell understanding" (ChatGPT). The Average-Joe copilot is
   a separate deliverable from alpha, and most of its analytics already exist
   in aegis-finance — the gap is holdings-first UX, not new math.

## Where they're wrong (and we push back)

- **Gemini: "the backtest will absolutely obliterate the S&P 500."** A promised
  outcome before the test is run is the exact fraud this project's architecture
  exists to prevent. Its agent-decomposition idea is fine; the guarantee is
  snake oil. Rejected.
- **DeepSeek: "Senate trades outperform 5-10% annually."** Overstated. The
  post-STOCK-Act literature finds little to no post-*disclosure* alpha on
  average (disclosure lags 30-45 days). We don't trust the meme either way —
  TRIAL-CONGRESS-IC is already accruing the forward answer.
- **ChatGPT's 98/100-style ratings** are flattery-shaped and carry no
  information. Its three substantive ideas are good, though: trust-weighted
  strategy ensemble (adopted at confirm/fusion stage), belief-propagation
  framing (model *changes in beliefs* — revisions, insider action, holdings —
  rather than price; this is literally what our surviving signals do), and
  episodic memory (partially exists as Optimus/postmortems; formalize later).
- **"Reward/punish RL allocator" (Murat + Gemini + DeepSeek).** On 276 monthly
  observations, an RL agent rewarded for beating SPY is an overfitting machine
  by construction. DEFERRED: revisit only as an explore-tier instrument after
  ≥2 confirmed survivors exist to allocate between.
- **"Just overfit 1980-now and show me 30-60%/yr."** We can always manufacture
  that number; it would be fiction and we will not present fiction as a
  result. The honest version is the explore/confirm split now running: free
  search on 2004-2018, untouched holdout 2019-2024, deflated confirm trials.
  (Also: current CRSP pull starts 2002; extending the panel to 1980 is one
  more WRDS pull and is on the roadmap for robustness-checking graduates.)

## What all three missed (our scars, not in their training data)

1. **Survivorship.** They propose scraping/free data for 40-year backtests.
   T7 (2026-06-16) proved free data cannot build a survivorship-free universe;
   EODHD failed its acceptance gate 14/20. Only CRSP-grade data counts.
2. **Turnover drag is the #1 killer.** BRAIN-001: naive monthly refresh costs
   ~45 bps/mo — most strategies on their lists die net of costs. Every factory
   scan runs with the hold-band and 25 bps one-way charges.
3. **Silent fragility.** A signal pipeline that runs green and quietly emits
   zeros corrupts everything downstream. House discipline (staleness guards,
   artifact contracts, leak bars) applies to every new collector they propose.
