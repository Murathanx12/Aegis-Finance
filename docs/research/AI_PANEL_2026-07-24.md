## The Murat-prompt adjudication

The prompt asks to "predict the future" (Iran → oil), "cover more data," and
"download all data and ingest it to the brain." Against the ledger:

- **Event prediction**: rejected by all four AIs *and* by the house's own three
  crash-timing rejects. What survives is reaction systematization → items 2–5.
- **More data**: the recon shows the binding constraint is not data volume —
  16,195 FDA events sit on disk unusable for want of a sponsor→ticker
  crosswalk, and the supplier links need name-resolution. The next unlock is
  entity resolution and event timestamps, not new downloads (GPT's point 10).
- **"Buy the dip in a crash, hold SPY otherwise"**: the honest form is item 12
  (quality-conditioned deployment) + the trend/vol overlays (items 2–3). The
  unconditional form is batch-1-rejected.
